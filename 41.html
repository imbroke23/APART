41. Seated Boxer. Hellenistic Greek. 100 BCE Bronze

  • Form
    • older man, past his prime, defeated look
    • Smashed nose; lips sunken in to suggest broken teeth
    • Cauliflower ears
    • Nude fighters with wrapped hands
    • Blood denoted in copper, from face and onto right arm & thigh
    • Copper used as highlights on lips, nipples, straps on leather gloves, wounds on head
    • Great emotion
  • Function
    • May have been a good luck charm for athletes (evidence: toes worn away by touching)
    • May have been part of a group sculpture, turned face to opponent
