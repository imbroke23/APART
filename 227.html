227. Summer trees. Song Su-Nam. 1983 CE Ink of paper.

Form

-Large vertical lines of various thicknesses -Subtle tonal variations of ink wash

Content

--Song Su-Nam was a Korean artist who used traditional ink on paper. -The artist was one of the leaders of the Sumukhwa, a new type of ink brush painting in the 1980s -Ink painting is a traditional form of artistic expression in Korea; this movement revitalizes ink painting in a modern context -Inspired by Western abstraction.
