Chapter 1 Art Before History

Concepts/ Main Ideas

-Human expression existed across the globe before written records -Very early art was produced worldwide and shared certain features- particularly concerned with the natural world and humans’ place within it- art provides insight into the beginnings of ritual life -1st instances of important artistic media, approaches, and values that occurred on different continents- Africa and Asia precede and influence other areas - Prehistoric (Paleolithic) hunters attributed magical properties to the images they painted - In Neolithic times human beings began to settle into villages and domesticate animals and food grains- this changed Neolithic man’s view of himself and the world.
