37. Winged Victory Samothrace. Hellenistic Greek. 190 BCE. Marble

  • Form
    • Wet drapery look imitates the water playing on the wet body
    • Shows evidence of invisible wind on her body
    • Dramatic twist & contrapposto of torso
    • Missing right may have:
      • arm raised a victory crown
      • Held an open hand in greeting
      • Landing on the prow of the ship
      • The boat at the base is a battleship with oarboxes and traces of a ram
  • Function
    • Meant to stand in or above a fountain cascading water around rocks below (figure head of a boat)
    • Probably built to commemorate a naval victory in 190 BCE
